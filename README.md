##  About Me  
Hi! I'm **Fujii**, a creative developer blending **web development**, **3D art**,  
**game creation**, and **cinematic editing** into a single artistic identity.

I love building expressive digital experiences — from modern UIs to stylized 3D,  
from engaging videos to atmospheric game worlds.

---

##  What I Do

###  Web Development  
- Modern, stylish UIs (Discord-style, interactive art sites)  
- React + Tailwind + JavaScript  
- School management systems & custom dashboards  

###  3D Art (Blender)  
- Procedural buildings  
- Fancy stylized compositions  
- Geometry nodes & animation  

###  Editing (DaVinci Resolve)  
- YouTube video editing  
- Good timeline management  
- Smooth transitions + motion graphics  

###  Game Development  
- Unreal Engine  
- Open-world scenes  
- Horror & atmosphere-driven games
